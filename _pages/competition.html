Competition

2023 April Competition
The next UAV Chase Challenge will be held on April 21 and 22, 2023. The April competition is also a two-stage competition. The first stage is online only by a simulator and will start on January 3, 2023 with final submissions on February 28, 2023. The finalists from the simulator round will be announced on March 8, 2023 and be invited to Purdue campus (travel grants are available for up to $2,000 per team). The finalists will launch their UAVs (must be based on the PX4 frame, vehicles will be provided) on April 21 and 22. Please join the slack channel for any help, questions, or updates.
