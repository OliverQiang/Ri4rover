Competition

2024 July Maze Competition
The 2024 IEEE Maze Competition will be held on July 2024. This competition requires contestants to create control software for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) or ground-based rovers to autonomously enter a maze and find the exit. The competition contains two phases: the first phase is an online simulator and will start on February 1, 2024 with final submissions due by the end of day anywhere on Earth (AOE) on May 1, 2024. The finalists from the simulator round will be announced on May 8, 2024. The second phase is in-person where the competitors can demonstrate their solutions. The winners from the first phase will be invited to Purdue University to demonstrate their solutions and compete for cash prizes on July 19-21. Please join the slack channel for any help, questions, or updates.
